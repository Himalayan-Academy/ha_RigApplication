Welcome to the Master Course Student Resource Center, where you can share questions, testimonies, see news of interest and read inspirational insights from Satguru Bodhinatha Veylanswami.

Master Course Forum - Students who are actively participating and currently enrolled in the formal self-evaluation course will be invited to join the Master Course Students private Facebook group after they send in at least one self-evaluation worksheet. In the Facebook group you can post questions and testimonies, or share links, photos and videos that you think would be of interest to the group. You will need a personal Facebook account to join the group.

[FYI "Alumni" or students who decide to discontinue their study will be removed from the membership over time.]

The OnLine Lexicon is a excellent resource for answering questions. If you do not understand some words or would like to know more, just go to the online lexicon enter a word such as "karma" then follow all the "see also" entries for a mind-expanding walk through the garden of Saivite Hindu thought.
