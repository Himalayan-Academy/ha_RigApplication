For Level Two Students of the Master Course

For non-Hindus and non-Saivites who will be changing their names to progress in the Himalayan Academy Maste Course study, we provide here a list of last names. Make three choices and send those to Satguru Bodhinatha to choose from.

For those who have discovered this page while surfing the web, you can find out more by clicking on the links above under "Study Programs" and reading the book How to Become (a Better) Hindu. available at our book store.
