2001 Northern Europe and Russia Innersearch

A historic 12-day cruise with Gurudeva on the MS Amsterdam

Imagine spending 12 days with one of the greatest spiritual leaders of this century while experiencing the grandness of Northern Europe and Russia. A time and place where dreams converge with reality. A journey unlike anything you've ever experienced. Satguru Sivaya Subramuniyaswami led this exotic educational and spiritual voyage himself, and it was one of the final journeys of his amazing life. 71 Innersearchers joined Gurudeva on this truly historic voyage through 8 countries. For this fortunate group, it was a journey never to be forgotten!
Pre-Innersearch - Hamm & London
Harwich, Oslo & Helsingborg | Warnemunde & Ronne
Stockholm & Helsinki | St. Petersburg | Talinn & the Baltic Sea
Post Innersearch - Brande & Herning
