Our 2006 Australia New Zealand Innersearch saw 45 pilgrims join Bodhinatha on this 14-day adventure. The journey took us across the great southern lands, traversing it by planes, trains and prairie schooners (known to city-dwellers as busses).

The journey also engaged us with the local Hindu communities. From the Aussie outback to the heights of the mighty Kiwi Alps, we enjoyed the sweet company of Bodhinatha in classes and fun excursions.
