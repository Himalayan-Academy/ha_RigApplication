Cancellation and Refund Terms

Notice of cancellation must be submitted in writing, either by email to innersearch@hindu.org or by mail to Innersearch Travel-Study Programs, Himalayan Academy, 107 Kaholalele Road, Kapaa, HI 96746 USA.

If your notice of cancellation is timestamped or postmarked on or before May 2, 2014, you will be refunded 100% of fees paid less a US$250 cancellation fee.

If your notice of cancellation is timestamped or postmarked after May 2, 2014, you will be refunded 100% of fees paid less a US$1,000 cancellation fee and all expenses and deposits made on behalf of participant which are not refunded to Himalayan Academy.
