Innersearch Mauritius 2014

E-news Update for August 23, 2014

Namaste Innersearchers!

This will most likely be our final Innersearch Mauritius 2014 e-news to all of you.

Once again, thank you all for a wonderful program! We have been hearing nothing but positive and loving feedback about how this Innersearch has changed the lives of all those involved.

You will be happy to know that we have completed a collection of media from the program to share with you.

A Dropbox folder has been set up with select photos taken by the staff throughout the program, including a subfolder containing the family and individual portrait shots taken with Bodhinatha. You’ll also see a few audio files.

If there are any events for which you would like to see more photos, just ask, and we will place more in the Dropbox folder. We also have a good amount of unedited video footage. Should you desire video from a certain event, let us know and we’ll do our best to provide it in a reasonable amount of time.

For those of you interested in the iPad apps that Saravananathaswami presented during the meditation classes, he has posted a YouTube video of the chakras and simshumbisi illustration made in the Forge of Neon app. This can be used as a guide to create the illustration yourself on your iPad.
