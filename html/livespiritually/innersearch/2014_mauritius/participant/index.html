Innersearch Mauritius 2014

Basic Information

Please check back here occasionally for updates. We will be sure to mark what is NEW so that it is obvious.

Preparation

As part of our preparation for the journey, we are encouraging everyone to start reading Gurudeva's daily Master Course lessons, if you are not doing so already. The daily lessons will help you set a nice foundation to have some wonderful inner experiences in the classes and temples in Mauritius. They will also bring you into the inner-plane schools at night as the program approaches. You may read the lessons from the books if you have them, online, or you may subscribe to the free daily e-mail. (On the e-mail subscription page, you may wish to uncheck all the options except "The Master Course," unless, of course, you would like to subscribe to the other newsletters.)

We encourage everyone to join our Innersearch Facebook Group to begin to get to know your fellow participants. The Facebook Group is also a place where you can connect with other participants coming from your area. Mauritius may be very far away from where you live; it may be possible, not to mention fun, to arrange for travel along the same route and dates as other participants from your country or region.

Fitness Guidelines

In order to participate fully in this journey you will need an average level of fitness. A typical day will see us rising early for hatha yoga, then sharing morning and afternoon classes, lunch and dinner together. The nature of the journey involves traveling on some days by bus and boats. You will generally find your days fully scheduled and fairly long.

Health

You should be in good health to satisfactorily participate in this tour. Please advise Himalayan Academy of any pre-existing medical conditions which may limit or prohibit your full participation. All applicants should consider their personal ability and comfort levels with regards to the itinerary. Please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss the nature of the journey and any concerns you may have.

Vaccinations

Health information for travelers to Mauritius from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention may be found at http://bit.ly/mauritius-vaccinations

We recommend that you contact your doctor or a recognized travel health specialist for professional up-to-date advice, ideally 4-6 weeks before your trip.

Diet

All meals are vegetarian. We will do our best, but, unfortunately, we cannot guarantee that special requirements can always be met. If you have critical, special dietary requirements, we recommend you bring a small supply of specialized foods from home to supplement your diet.

Ground Rules (UPDATED)

  1. No smoking during group activities or at any time at the Spiritual Park. At the hotel, please take care to obey signage indicating where smoking may be allowed or disallowed.
  2. No hard liquor. Wine and beer are OK, however, no alcohol will be served during group meals. It is okay to have alcohol on your own, later, and on your own bill. Extra drinks ordered during group meals will not be covered by the Innersearch.
  3. No illegal drugs.
  4. No shorts, short-dresses or tank tops. Long pants or dresses and kurta or polo shirts are encouraged for excursions.
  5. Hindu dress is strongly encouraged for classes with the swamis and temple events.
  6. If you visit the hotel pool, please wear modest attire (no bikinis).
