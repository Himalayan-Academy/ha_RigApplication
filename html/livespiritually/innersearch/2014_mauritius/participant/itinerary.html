Innersearch Mauritius 2014: Information for Participants

Itinerary

The welcoming and orientation to kick off the program will be held at 5:00 pm on July 2, and the last activity will end at 10:00 am on July 14. The program in between will be revealed as we go along, and will include classes with Bodhinatha and the swamis, worship and sadhanas at our Spiritual Park and some of the beautiful temples in the country, and spiritually-oriented outings.

July 2

Please arrange for an inbound flight that arrives no later than 2:00 pm to allow enough time for immigration, baggage collection and customs, followed by transportation to the hotel, so that you will arrive in time to attend the first event of the program. Our program will begin this evening with our traditional "Crow's Nest" orientation gathering at 5:00 pm in the hotel's Conference Room, followed by dinner.

July 3-4

We will dive right in to two full days of classes at our Spiritual Park on a variety of religious and spiritual subjects led by Satguru Bodhinatha Veylanswami and the swamis traveling with him on the program.

July 5

An exciting outing and event are planned for this day, the details of which will be kept secret until the night before!

July 6

Thousands of people from all over the island will descend upon the Spiritual Park for our monthly Ganesha Homa, a grand ceremony centered on the beautiful Panchamukha Ganapati Mandapam, the centerpiece of the property.

July 7-8

Two more days of classes at our Spiritual Park

July 9

Another outing is planned, including fun adventures to imbibe the beauty of this tropical island.

July 10-11

We dive back in to two final days of classes with Bodhinatha and his swamis, rounding out our experience, asking questions and learning many tools we can use to apply our new knowledge in our daily lives back home.

July 12

This is another special day, Satguru Purnima, the festival honoring gurus of all Hindu traditions and lineages. This year for the first time, Satguru Bodhinatha Veylanswami will be present at the Spiritual Park for the grand annual celebration held here in honor of him and the Nandinatha Sampradaya's Kailasa Parampara, the lineage of Saiva Siddhanta Church and Kauai's Hindu Monastery that began with Maharishi Nandinatha over 2,200 years ago and which was passed on to Bodhinatha in 2001 by his guru, Satguru Sivaya Subramuniyaswami, who first visited Mauritius in the early 1980s.

July 13

One more day of program activities, plus our Grand Finale Banquet, to be attended by all our Mauritius members.

July 14

The program will conclude with our traditional Aloha Breakfast, ending at 10:00 am. Hotel check-out time is 12:00 pm. Breakfast on this day is the last meal that is provided by the program. Please arrange for an outbound flight that departs no earlier than 2:00 pm on July 14 so that you will be able to attend the entire closing event of the program and allow enough time for transportation to the airport, check-in, security checkpoint and boarding.
